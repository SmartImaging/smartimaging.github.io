From 2010-2024, the SmartImaging Lab lead by Dr. Huijun Chen started a series of work on MRI quantification, including MRI sequence design, MRI reconstruction and deep learning image analysis. Students were selected from universities internationally.

SmartImaging Lab fellows worked closely with each other to design and integrate a MR pipeline ready for clinical transition.
