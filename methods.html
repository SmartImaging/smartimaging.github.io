The Smart Imaging Lab, part of Center for Biomedical Imaging Research (CBIR), primarily focuses on quantification of medical images through mathematical modeling, computational modeling, and deep learning. This process of imaging parameter and post-processing quantification, machine learning post-processing manipulation was defined smart imaging by our lab. We apply these advanced techniques to a wide range of diseases, with particular emphasis on carotid artery atherosclerosis, liver cancer, and heart diseases.
